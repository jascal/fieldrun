Abstract

We decompile small transformer language models into a flat retrieval store (n-gram, induction, grammar) plus a composition kernel, and use the decomposition to ask a sharp, measurable question of every next-token decision: is the model retrieving a symbolic rule, selecting within a rule-proposed set, or computing something new? Across two Qwen2.5-0.5B models and the four-rung Pythia ladder (70M–1B), the labour splits roughly 25% retrieved, 60% selected, 15% composed: a small but irreducible core of genuinely computed tokens, the irreducible computation, that grows with scale. The mechanism is not selection by magnitude: the predicted logit is a uniformly-distributed ∼45-way additive sum (participation ratio PR ≈ 45, route-invariant). Retrieval versus computation instead separates cleanly on two near-orthogonal, exactly measurable axes: a normalised power-diagram margin (the Euclidean distance from the residual to the nearest unembedding facet) and a single-circuit readout multiplicity µt . Composed tokens are emergent (the argmax of the sum but of no summand) and causally fragile under ablation, with a repair term that is provably diffuse (P(single-module rescue) ≈ 1/PR). We then give one theory in three interpretations. It accounts for these measurements and turns out to be a single object at two temperatures. As a probabilistic logic (temperature one) it is Bundy’s incidence calculus with two assumptions removed: incidences become signed measures in an innerproduct space and Boolean ∩/∪ becomes a weighted threshold, with the Gram kernel Gvw = ⟨Uv , Uw ⟩ as the explicit carrier of non-truth-functionality. As a geometry (temperature zero) it is the tropical variety of the decision surface, and the irreducible computation is a tropical-rank floor that linear rank cannot see. As a computation it is a semiring-weighted Datalog program whose two temperatures are sum-product (the softmax) and max-product (the greedy decode) over one program; Maslov dequantization is the homomorphism between them. The retrievable fragment exports to compact, verifiable Datalog; the irreducible computation is exactly the dense-Gram, high-treewidth, high-tropical-rank region that no lookup table reproduces. 1. Introduction

A large language model, asked for the next token, sometimes does something a lookup table could do and sometimes does not. “ The capital of France is ” is answered by retrieval; the model has effectively memorised a fact and the harder question is only where it is stored [11, 19]. But many tokens are not in any table the model could be said to consult: they are computed on the fly, assembled from a distributed interaction of partial contributions. The central empirical question of this paper is how to tell these apart operationally, on a real model, token by token, and what the mechanism of each looks like. We attack the question with a decompiler. The fieldrun toolchain splits a small transformer into two halves that together reproduce it: a flat retrieval store (n-gram successor tables, induction copy-rules, a closed-class grammar skeleton) that reconstructs roughly half of the model’s predictions with no linear algebra at all, and a composition kernel that runs the attention+MLP forward pass as plain matrix multiplies over flat weight arrays [23]. Every weight tier is validated by exact top-1 agreement against a reference implementation, so the decomposition is faithful rather than approximate. Given the retrieval store’s candidate set for a context, we classify the model’s argmax token t into three routes: RETRIEVED (a single store idiom’s top-1 is t, a pure symbolic lookup), SELECTED (t is in the candidate set but is no idiom’s top1; the set contains the answer, the choice within it is made elsewhere), and COMPOSED (t is in no rule’s output, the irreducible computation, genuinely computed). The headline measurement is stable and, we will argue, theoretically loaded. Across two Qwen2.5-0.5B models [24] and all four rungs of the Pythia ladder [2], the model’s labour divides roughly 25% RETRIEVED, 60% SELECTED, 15% COMPOSED . Composition is mostly disambiguation within a retrieved set, not generation from nothing; only the ∼15% irreducible computation is from scratch, and it grows monoton1

ically with model scale against a fixed store. Three further facts sharpen the picture and resist the obvious explanations. First, the route split is not a magnitude distinction: the predicted logit is a uniformly distributed ∼45-way additive sum (participation ratio PR ≈ 45, route-invariant), so no circuit ever “decides” a token by dominating the sum, not even one a single n-gram rule reproduces perfectly. Second, the split separates cleanly on two other, near-orthogonal axes that we can measure exactly: a decision margin that is the Euclidean distance from the residual stream to the nearest unembedding power-diagram facet, and a single-circuit readout multiplicity µt . Third, composed tokens are emergent (the argmax of the sum but of no summand, µt ≈ 0) and causally fragile under ablation, with a repair mechanism that is provably diffuse rather than localisable. These measurements ask for a theory, and the theory we give is one object seen from three sides. The thread that ties them is older than transformers: it is Alan Bundy’s incidence calculus [4], invented in 1985 precisely to reason with probabilities that are not truth-functional by tracking the underlying incidences (the worlds in which a proposition holds) and recovering probability as a measure on them. We make three claims, each an interpretation of the same structure: 1. As a probabilistic logic at temperature T = 1 (the soft accumulation and the recovered output measure), the core is incidence calculus with exactly two of Bundy’s assumptions removed. Incidences become signed measures in an inner-product space rather than sets, and Boolean ∩/∪ become a weighted threshold. The Gram kernel Gvw = ⟨Uv , Uw ⟩ of the unembedding frame is the explicit operator carrying non-truth-functionality; the classical Boolean calculus is recovered exactly as the diagonal-G limit. 2. As a geometry at temperature T = 0 (the hard argmax, its cells, margins and rank), the decision surface is the tropical variety of a max-logit polynomial whose monomials are the unembedding rows. Composition is interior (non-monomial) tropical points, and the irreducible computation is a tropical-rank floor: the gap between the model’s tropical rank and that of any flat lookup table, a gap that linear (SVD) rank structurally cannot measure. 3. As a computation (the executable, statically checkable artifact), the core is a semiring-weighted Datalog program Π. Evaluated under the log-semiring it is sum-product and returns the softmax distribution; under the tropical semiring it is max-product and returns the greedy decode. The two temperatures are one program under two semirings, and Maslov dequantization is the homomorphism between them. The unification is not loose analogy. The same kernel G that hardens near-synonym competition in the logic is the facet geometry in the tropical picture and the provenance coupling in the Datalog picture; the same irreducible computation is the high participation-ratio residual, the high tropical rank, and

the high-treewidth dense-G region; the same temperature parameter is the semiring choice. We present the measurements first (Sections 3–4), because they constrain every axiom of the theory, and then the three interpretations and their unification (Section 5), the reproduction recipe (Section 6), and the open frontier (Section 7). The tribute is plain throughout: this is Bundy’s calculus, forty years on, meeting a substrate he could not have had.

2. Background and Related Work

2.1 Mechanistic interpretability

Our measurement apparatus inherits the circuits program’s central move: read computation off the model in the basis of its own weights. The residual stream is a linear sum of component writes, so a final-layer logit decomposes additively over heads and neurons; the direct logit attribution (DLA) of a component to token v is ⟨d j , Uv ⟩, the inner product of the component’s write d j with the unembedding row Uv [10]. Superposition, the storage of more features than dimensions as a near-orthogonal overcomplete code, predicts exactly the distributed, low-percircuit-magnitude readout we measure [9], and sparse autoencoders attempt to recover that code as monosemantic features [3, 7]. Induction heads, the copy-from-earlier-match mechanism behind much in-context learning [20], are the one idiom that exports as a clean recursive rule (Section 5). Feed-forward layers as key–value memories [11] and the locate-and-edit line [19] are the retrieval end of the spectrum we are trying to delimit from the computed end. Where this work differs is in making the retrieve-versus-compute distinction a measured, per-token classification with an exact geometric and causal characterisation, rather than a case study of one circuit.

2.2 Incidence calculus

Incidence calculus [4, 5] was built on one load-bearing observation: probability is not truth-functional, since P(A ∧ B) is not a function of P(A) and P(B), but incidence is. Bundy attaches to each proposition A a set i(A) ⊆ I of “incidences” (possible worlds, or sampled situations) in which A holds, makes the connectives set operations (i(A ∧ B) = i(A) ∩ i(B)), and recovers probability as the measure P(A) = |i(A)|/|I|. The joint that probability loses is carried explicitly by the set overlap. Our claim is that the transformer’s composition core is this calculus with the orthogonality assumption removed: its propositions are not Boolean indicator sets but directions in an inner-product space whose pairwise overlaps are a dense Gram kernel, and its connective is not intersection but a weighted threshold. The classical calculus reappears, exactly, when that kernel is diagonal.

2.3 Log-linear, tropical, and semiring views

The additive core is a log-linear / product-of-experts model [13, 15], and Markov logic networks [21] are its closest symbolicprobabilistic cousin; what we add is the inner-product (frameGram) coupling and the explicit retrievable/computed split.

The hard decision surface is tropical: an argmax over linear forms is the (max, +) semiring, and the ReLU/PWL-networkto-tropical lineage [25, 17] gives the decision map as a tropical rational function whose linear-region count is bounded by Newton polytope vertices. Tropical rank [8] supplies the irreducible-computation floor; power (Laguerre) diagrams [1] are the decision cells. The bridge between the soft (T = 1) and hard (T = 0) pictures is Maslov dequantization, the T → 0 limit of the log-semiring into (max, +) [16]. The executable form lives in provenance / semiring Datalog [12, 14, 6]: sumproduct over a semiring is database evaluation, and the temperature is the semiring parameter. The correlated-alternatives regime connects to discrete-choice econometrics (GEV, nested logit) [18]. Finally, the phenomenon of “redundant distributed voting shading to emergent combination” has ingredients with precedent (distributed connectionist production systems [22], superposition, products of experts, and incidence calculus over a continuous learned space), but, as far as we are aware, the fusion is new.

3. The Decompiler and the Measurement

3.1 The two-tier decompile

fieldrun is a single static binary that runs a model from a flat bundle (a raw weight blob plus a small manifest) with no deeplearning framework at runtime. The build side decompiles a model into Tier A, a retrieval store (induction thresholds; quad/tri/bi/uni n-gram successor tables; an optional closedclass grammar skeleton), and Tier B, the attention+MLP composition kernel as plain matmuls. Faithfulness is enforced by a top-1 gate: Tier A reproduces the reference idiom predictions with zero per-position mismatches over 500 positions, and Tier B matches the numpy/torch reference exactly in f32 (e.g. GPT-2 200/200, Qwen2.5 32/32). All probes below are explain-only: the decode path is untouched, so there is no faithfulness-gate risk and the numbers describe the real model, not a surrogate.

3.2 Objects

For a position, let r ∈ Rd be the final residual stream; the model’s logits are Lv = ⟨r, Uv ⟩ + bv and its prediction is t = arg maxv Lv . 3.3 Models, holdouts, probes

The primary study uses Qwen2.5-Coder-0.5B-Instruct and Qwen2.5-0.5B-Instruct (shared vocabulary, hence a shared model-captured store), on a natural-text and a code holdout, ∼300–500 contexts at context window 64. The cross-architecture replication uses the Pythia ladder (70M/160M/410M/1B) under a corpus n-gram store held constant across architectures. The probes, each a fieldrun CLI mode, are: --attribute (the three-way route split), --probe (is selection a function of the firing state?), --probe-dla (PR, margin, µt ), --probe-facet (exact nearest power-diagram facet over the full vocabulary), --probe-ablate (singleand multi-circuit causal ablation), --probe-reconstruct (perblock logit reconstruction), and --probe-quant (per-block quantisation sensitivity). Numbers are indicative (n∼300), not high-precision; the qualitative structure is what replicates.

4. Empirical Findings

4.1 The irreducible computation: a measured three-way split

Classifying each next-token decision by route gives, on natural text, roughly 25% RETRIEVED, 60% SELECTED, 15% COMPOSED: the model’s labour is retrieval-dominated, and most of what looks like “computation” is disambiguation within a retrieved candidate set. The genuinely-computed irreducible computation is the ∼15% (the COMPOSED route). The split is regime-dependent in the expected direction: at ∼540 store candidates, the candidate set covers the model’s argmax ∼85% of the time on natural text but only ∼63% on code, because code is computed, not retrieved. Table 1 shows the split is robust across a second architecture family and a different store construction.

On the Pythia ladder, PR runs 13 → 27 → 63 → 36 (70M/160M/410M/1B): not monotone in parameters, but monotone in head count (48/144/384/128). The “∼45-way sum” is thus an architecture-shape parameter (how many circuits exist to spread over), not a universal constant.

4.3 The two separating axes: margin and readout multiplicity

If magnitude does not separate the routes, two other quantities do, and they are nearly independent (per-position correlation r ≈ 0.15, ∼2% shared variance). The first is geometry: the exact nearest power-diagram facet distance is monotone RETRIEVED ≫ SELECTED > COMPOSED (coder 2.23/1.34/1.03, instruct 2.78/1.45/1.22; same shape on every Pythia rung), so retrieved tokens sit deep inside their decision cell and composed tokens sit near a facet. The second is redundancy: the readout multiplicity µt . Coverable tokens are redundantly multiply-realised (SELECTED mean µt = 1.45/1.06), while ∼80% of COMPOSED tokens are strictly emergent: µt = 0, the answer is the argmax of the ∼45-way sum but of no summand (COMPOSED µt =0 fraction 84%/76%). This is the operational definition of emergence we will formalise three ways in Section 5: the argmax of a sum that is the argmax of no summand.

The split survives a confidence control: within matched margin bins, the covered-versus-composed redundancy gap persists (coder 65/71% vs 17/16%), so it is not merely that the store covers the model’s confident predictions. A second, geometric critical test rules out the most natural story for composition. One might guess that a composed token is the residual r crossing the facet out of the store’s predicted cell. It is not: the nearest facet is the bisector with the store’s own prediction for only 14%/8% of COMPOSED tokens. For ∼85% of composed tokens the store’s prediction is not even the nearest competitor, so composition is a non-local divergence from the rule, not a near-miss. The ∼14% near-miss subclass is one identifiable thing: closed-class and morphological coin-flips (a ⊥the, will ⊥ is, ;⊥,, -ful ⊥ -y), the grammar regime where the store is strongest, not novel computation.

4.4 Causal ablation: composed is fragile, and the repair is diffuse

Turning the readout into an intervention, zeroing the top-DLA circuit in the forward pass and asking whether the prediction flips, converts these correlations into causal structure. Five results stand out. (i) Route-ordered fragility, margin-governed. flip@k=1 is RETRIEVED 22/26% < SELECTED 40/48% < COMPOSED 54/61%: knock out the single top circuit and a composed token flips ∼2.4× as often as a retrieved one. But this tracks the margin, and a margin-matched split shows the margin is the governor: flips collapse 76 → 41 → 17% across margin terciles identically for µt ≥2 and µt =0 tokens. (ii) Redundancy is non-compensatory. Holding the ablated circuit fixed to a confirmed t-supporter and splitting on whether a backup remains (µt =1 vs µt ≥2), backups confer no robust protection (pooled flip: low-∆ 90%/80%, high-∆ 4%/21%). Removing one supporter is not caught by the others: apparent agreement among many weak readers is not fault tolerance. The redundant supporters, individually < 10% of the logit at PR ≈ 40, provide essentially no cushion. (iii) The causal variable is pivotality D j versus margin ∆, not µt . The linear flip identity flip ⇐⇒ ∆ < D j holds as a near-perfect necessary condition: sign(D j − ∆) mispredicts a non-flip only 3/300 (coder) and 17/300 (instruct) times. A logistic control flip ∼ ∆ + D j + 1[µt ≥2] gives standardised weights ∆ = −4.21/ − 3.11, D j = +2.82/ + 1.16, and µt essentially zero (−0.60/ + 0.06, opposite signs across models); dropping µt costs +0.0035/ + 0.000 mean log-loss. So µt is a proxy for (∆, D j ) position, not an independent cause. (iv) Non-truth-functionality is a kernel. With ρ = cos(Ut ,Uv∗ ) the runner-up coherence, pivotality and flip rate both fall as competitors become near-synonyms (coder |D j | 1.47 → 0.86, flip 53 → 18%): common-mode evidence cancels

in D j = ⟨d j , Ut −Uv∗ ⟩. The competition geometry is read directly off the frame Gram, the structural fact Section 5.1 builds on. (v) The repair is diffuse, not a lever. When the linear identity predicts a flip, an indirect downstream recomposition rescues the original token about half the time, and this cushion scales with the margin (rescue 14 → 39 → 50 → 61% across ∆). But the rescue is not localisable: 18–34% of rescues survive ablating every single downstream block, and the per-head un-rescue rate is order-1/PR: measured 4.0–4.1% against 1/PR = 2.5–2.8%, a ∼1.5× constant. There is no surgical repair target; the repair is distributed for the same reason the readout is (PR ≈ 45).

4.5 Per-block reconstruction: exact, block-sparse, circuitdense

Decomposing the predicting logit into its 49 per-block residual writes (embedding + attention + MLP per layer) and checking ∑blocks = Lt gives floating-point exact reconstruction (mean error 5.9 × 10−6 /7.2 × 10−6 ). Residual-stream additivity holds, so the static decomposition reconstructs every logit exactly, the empirical face of the soundness theorem of Section 5.3. The decision is block-sparse (effective supporting blocks ≈ 8–10 of 49; top block to flip σ ≈ 1.1–1.6) but circuit-dense within a block (PR ≈ 45 over a block’s ∼14 heads). The readable program is compact at block granularity and bottoms out there for composed tokens; below the block it is the dense computed remainder.

4.6 Scaling and a research-to-speed bridge

On the Pythia ladder the robust core (the three-way split, routeinvariant PR, the RETRIEVED ≫ {SELECTED, COMPOSED} margin geometry, the refuted critical test, and margin-governed fragility) replicates at every scale. Two findings are scaledependent. The irreducible computation grows with scale (7.8 → 14.8% up the ladder against the same store; Figure 1): bigger models compute more. And µt -redundancy is something models grow into: at 70M/160M, µt ≈ 0 everywhere, while the full transition appears between 160M and 410M. The power-diagram geometry (margin) is scalestable from 70M; the readout multiplicity is scale-emergent then stable, consistent with µt being a readout property, not the causal variable. Finally, quantisation sensitivity tracks the split: single-block int4 flip rates are RETRIEVED 3.6/2.4% ≪ COMPOSED 5.9/18.8%, so the retrievable tier survives aggressive quantisation while the irreducible computation is where quantisation error concentrates. This is the research result with a deployment consequence (quantise the retrievable tier hard, protect the computed core).

5. One Theory in Three Interpretations

The measurements above constrain a theory tightly. It must be additive (the logit is an exact sum, Section 4.5); cardinalityinert (the count µt has no causal weight, only D j and ∆ do, Sec-

tion 4.4); carry a non-truth-functional coupling that hardens with ρ = cos(Ut ,Uv∗ ); have a weighted-threshold decision beyond Horn clauses (µt =0 for most composed tokens); and possess a second, diffuse, non-localisable repair layer bounded by 1/PR. We now give a structure with all five properties, and observe that it is a single object viewed as logic, geometry, or computation, according to a temperature/semiring parameter.

5.1 Interpretation I, Logic (T =1): Projective Incidence Calculus

Objects. Sources S (circuits) are vectors d j in an innerproduct space H ; propositions V (tokens) are directions Uv ∈ H . The projective pairing cvj = ⟨d j , Uv ⟩ is the direct logit attribution; aggregated evidence is the residual r = ∑ j d j with logits Lv = ⟨r, Uv ⟩ = ∑ j cvj . Two objects carry the theory. The Gram kernel Gvw = ⟨Uv , Uw ⟩ is the proposition frame’s inner-product structure, and the differential incidence Dt,v j = ⟨d j , Ut − Uv ⟩ is the atomic causal quantity. Where Bundy’s incidence of a proposition is a set i(v) ⊆ I, ours is the linear functional ⟨·, Uv ⟩, and the overlap of two propositions is not a set intersection but the kernel value Gvw . This is the central move: propositions share an inner-product space, so their incidences overlap intrinsically, and the structure that probability is not truth-functional over is carried by G. Connectives. Combination is signed linear accumulation in log-space, Lv = ∑ j cvj , equivalently a product of experts, each source multiplying a proposition’s mass by exp(cvj ) [13]. The decision is a weighted threshold: arg maxv Lv is the Laguerre power diagram of {Uv } with weights ∥Uv ∥2 , and the normalised margin is the facet distance (Section 5.2). The inferential analogue of incidence-calculus bounding is a coalition bound: from a known subset S′ ⊆ S, the decision margin is bounded by the partial sum ∑ j∈S′ D j plus a residual bound; the measured coalition additivity (sign ∑ j D j predicts jointablation flips at ∼75–83%) is its empirical face. Theorems. The desiderata are recovered as consequences, not posited. Theorem 1 (Cardinality-inertness). Under the projective pairing, the decision depends only on {D j , ∆} and is invariant to the count µt . This recovers the measured fact that µt carries no causal weight given (∆, D j ). Theorem 2 (Non-truth-functionality budget). Competition hardness √ between t and v is a monotone function of ρtv = Gtv / Gtt Gvv ; as ρ → 1, the differential incidence D j = ⟨d j , Ut −Uv ⟩ becomes common-mode and collapses. PIC reduces to classical Boolean incidence calculus exactly when G is diagonal. This recovers the measured ρ-boundary and identifies Bundy’s orthogonal case as the diagonal-G limit.

Theorem 3 (Weighted-threshold expressivity). COMPOSED conclusions (those with no single sufficient sub-conjunction, µt =0) are exactly the formulae not expressible in Horn / ∩-∪ incidence calculus but expressible with the weighted-threshold connective. Theorem 4 (Recovered probability). Maintain a measure over propositions with uniform base M0 ; let each source reweight every proposition’s mass by exp(cvj ). Then m(v) = M0 exp(∑ j cvj ) = M0 exp(Lv ) and m(v)/ ∑w m(w) = exp(Lv )/Z, exactly the model’s softmax. The Gibbs measure is recovered as a PIC incidence frequency, exactly and parameter-free. Theorem 4 is the direct descendant of Bundy’s “probability as proportion of worlds.” Crucially, G need not be injected as correlated noise (which would trade off exact recovery): it is already structural as the Gram of the proposition frame, so it structures the static logits (Theorem 4) and the competition geometry (Theorem 2) at once, with no variance trade-off. The second layer. PIC has a monotone additive core (the map r 7→ (Lv ) and the threshold) and a non-monotone fixpoint closure. Model the forward pass as an iterated operator T on the evidence vector; the additive core is its linearisation at the operating point, and the diffuse repair (the irreducible computation) is the higher-order closure T ∞ − (linearisation). It appears only under intervention (the off-diagonal Jacobian) and is provably diffuse in the high-PR regime: Theorem 5 (Diffuseness). Any causal property realised as E = ∑m em with equitable em ∼ E/PR has single-source influence O(1/PR); hence no bounded-size PIC formula localises it, and P(single-module intervention alters E) ≈ 1/PR. This recovers the measured per-head un-rescue rate (Section 4.4(v)), order-of-magnitude confirmed at 4.0–4.1% versus 1/PR = 2.5–2.8%. The ∼1.5× excess over 1/PR is consistent with a mild within-layer correlation of repair (a layer’s heads share rescue), the one place the equitable-contribution approximation is loosest. The clean one-line statement of the architecture: the recovered probability lives in the static decomposition (additive, exact); the irreducible computation lives in the intervention response (non-additive, diffuse); the same model exhibits both as different layers.

A flat retrieval table (“context key → stored next-token logits”) is a tropical map whose monomials are exactly its stored keys, one tropical term per row, tropical rank bounded by the table size. Composition is precisely the decision regions requiring monomials not in the table: sums of stored keys that create new interior cells. Writing ρtrop (core) for the tropical rank of the core’s decision map and ρtrop (KB) for the best flat table at matched coverage [8], the irreducible computation is exactly the unremovable part of ρtrop (core) − ρtrop (KB): the decision cells no lookup table reproduces because they are composed (interior) monomials. The measured COMPOSED fraction is the empirical shadow of this gap. This also explains why a linear (SVD) rank misranks the core: its hardness is the number of tropical monomials (decision cells), which a Frobenius rank does not measure, the same reason a dataaware low-rank update beats plain SVD at matched rank in the decompiler’s entangled-core experiments.

5.3 Interpretation III, Computation: Semiring-Weighted Datalog

The core’s next-token computation is semiring accumulation over a finite relational domain, evaluated bottom-up: contributions sum along the residual stream, then one competitive aggregation picks the token. That is exactly Datalog evaluated under a provenance semiring [12, 14]. We export the model as a semiring-weighted Datalog program Π: propositions V with directions Uv and Gram G; sources S with pairings cvj ; the retrievable fragment as compact stratified clauses (an induction head is a recursive clause next(T):-match_prefix(P),follows(P,T); an n-gram is a weighted fact; grammar is a unary constraint); the computed fragment as a flagged dense aggregate; and a provenance evaluator with a semiring/temperature parameter. Reading Π

as a Functional Aggregate Query, the decision is the aggregaL N tion v j (factor). Datalog rather than Prolog is a semantics decision: provenance semirings are a Datalog construct (Prolog has no semiring parameter, and cut / negation-as-failure / clause order break the semiring laws); forward residual accumulation matches Datalog’s bottom-up least-fixpoint; aggregation is native; and the program is decidable and statically analysable (PTIME data complexity, a unique least fixpoint): a mathematical object one can bound and verify, rather than a procedure. The retrievable rules are finite-domain over tokens and need none of Prolog’s extra power; the irreducible computation is dense arithmetic Prolog could not compact either. Theorem 6 (Two-temperature soundness). Read Π as a semiring FAQ, with sources combined by ⊗ along the residual N derivation (so the accumulated value of v is j cvj = ∑ j cvj = Lv ) and competing propositions combined by ⊕. Then • under the log-semiring (⊕ = log-sum-exp, ⊗ = +), [[Π]] is the sum-product: the aggregate is log ∑v exp(Lv ) = log Z and the per-proposition share is exp(Lv )/Z = P(v), the model’s softmax distribution (Interpretation I, T =1); • under the tropical semiring (⊕ = max, ⊗ = +), [[Π]] is the max-product: the aggregate is maxv Lv with witness arg maxv Lv , the model’s greedy decode (Interpretation II, T =0). This is the standard sum-product / max-product duality instantiated on Π, plus the product-of-experts recovery of Theorem 4; the log ↔ max homomorphism is Maslov dequantization, so the export is correct at both temperatures by one identity. It is confirmed numerically by the exact perblock reconstruction (Section 4.5): ∑blocks = Lt to floating point means the static export is faithful. The emitter is implemented: fieldrun export --logic writes a runnable, Soufflé-compatible semiring-Datalog program for one decode (candidate facts, the Tier-A retrievable clauses, perblock contrib facts with the dense remainder folded into a "rest" block, and the (max, +) decode), self-checking ∑ contrib = Lt and that the decode equals the model’s token; a built-in evaluator runs it under either semiring (eval --semiring max returns the token, --semiring log the distribution): one program, two semirings, two temperatures. The provenance gap. The clean theorem assumes independent factors; they are not, because Gvw = ⟨Uv , Uw ⟩ couples every pair. Hence: Conjecture 1 (Provenance gap). Faithful correlated evaluation of Π requires provenance valued in the frame geometry (carrying the Uv directions, or the operator G), not scalars in a commutative semiring over R. Scalar semiring Datalog is exact only on diagonal G (the orthogonal / classical-incidencecalculus case); for dense G, scalar provenance drops the joint exactly as classical incidence calculus drops i(A) ∩ i(B).

This is the same wall as the irreducible computation seen from the computation side: a dense G coupling is a hightreewidth factor graph, and sum-product is exponential in treewidth, so the correlated evaluation is at once intractable and non-compact. Solving the provenance gap would solve Interpretation I’s non-truth-functionality theorem; that one-toone correspondence is the strongest evidence the three interpretations are one theory.

5.4 The unification

The three interpretations are one object under a single degree of freedom, the temperature, which is the semiring choice. As T → 0, T log ∑v exp(Lv /T ) → maxv Lv and softmax(L/T ) → arg max, by Maslov dequantization [16]. The power diagram is limT →0 of the softmax cells; Interpretation I’s coherence kernel ρtv = cos(Ut ,Uv∗ ) becomes the tropical facet angle; Interpretation I’s smoothed competition is the T > 0 viscosity regularisation of the tropical variety. The same irreducible computation is the high-PR residual (Interpretation I), the high tropical rank (Interpretation II), and the high-treewidth denseG region (Interpretation III). One wall, three measures: participation ratio, tropical rank, treewidth. And the same kernel G is the non-truth-functionality operator, the facet geometry, and the provenance coupling. The retrievable fragment is, in all three, the additive / low-rank / low-treewidth part that exports to a compact verifiable program; the computed fragment is the diffuse / high-rank / dense-G remainder with no compact symbolic form. This is the program’s logic-programming thesis made precise: the model is a semiring-weighted Datalog program, decoding is provenance evaluation, and its two temperatures are the softmax measure and the greedy argmax over one program.

6. Reproducibility

Every quantitative claim traces to an explain-only probe in the fieldrun toolchain (the Apache-2.0 licensed source, bundles, and the exact holdout and store manifests behind Table 1 to be released with the camera-ready version), which runs a model from a flat bundle in pure Rust with no deep-learning framework. After converting a checkpoint (fieldrun convert --model <hf-id> --arch rope) and building a store, the probes reproduce the paper: # three-way route split (Sec. 5.1) fieldrun --bundle <m> --ids holdout.json \ --store store.json --attribute # PR, normalized margin, mu_t (Sec. 5.2-5.3) fieldrun ... --probe-dla --n-eval 500 # exact nearest power-diagram facet (Sec. 6.2) fieldrun ... --probe-facet # causal ablation; mu_t-falsifier; 1/PR lemma fieldrun ... --probe-ablate --n-eval 300 \ --head-sweep # per-block reconstruction: sum(blocks)==logit fieldrun ... --probe-reconstruct --n-eval 300 # quantization sensitivity by route (Sec. 5.7) fieldrun ... --probe-quant --bits 4 --n-eval 80

# emit a runnable semiring-Datalog program and # evaluate it under either semiring (Sec. 6.3) fieldrun ... export --logic --ctx 32 \ --candidates 24 --out decode.dl fieldrun eval decode.dl --semiring max # decode fieldrun eval decode.dl --semiring log # softmax

The cross-architecture replication (Table 1) uses the same probes on Pythia bundles (--arch neox) under a corpus ngram store, with the faithfulness gate validated top-1 against a pure-numpy reference. All probes leave the decode path untouched.

The lineage is the point. Incidence calculus was built in 1985 on the insight that probability is not truth-functional but incidence is, and that one recovers probability by measuring incidences [4]. Forty years on, a transformer’s composition core turns out to be exactly that calculus with the orthogonality assumption removed: its propositions live in an inner-product space whose Gram kernel is the explicit carrier of the nontruth-functional joint, its connective is a weighted threshold rather than Boolean intersection, and its output probabilities are recovered, exactly, as incidence frequencies. The classical calculus sits inside ours as the diagonal-G limit. We have only added a substrate, a thermometer, and a fixpoint engine; the central idea is Bundy’s.

7. Open Problems

The theory is anchored where it can be measured and conjectural exactly where the measurements stop. The sharpest open questions are: (O1) the soundness and completeness of weighted incidence resolution, the coalition bound ∑ j∈S′ D j as a sound inference rule; (O2) whether the support number σ (t) (the smallest sufficient circuit set) scales as the participation ratio, tying cardinality-inertness to the absence of a small sufficient body, (O3) a derivation of the measured ∼1.5× constant in P(single-module repair) = κ/PR from the fixpoint closure, accounting for the within-layer correlation of repair; (O4, the central one) a non-scalar, geometry-valued provenance semiring that carries G faithfully and reduces to the scalar log/tropical semiring on diagonal G (solving it closes the provenance gap and Interpretation I’s non-truth-functionality theorem at once); (O5) the treewidth of the core’s factor graph as a third quantitative irreducible-computation measure, related to the participation ratio and the tropical rank (one wall, three measures); and (O6) cross-scale invariance of G’s spectrum and the retrievable/computed split as a program-wide thesis. A PIC syntax (compiling formulae directly from DLA traces, with composed positions flagged as “no compact formula”) would make the retrievable fragment a statically verifiable extracted program and the irreducible computation a named, bounded region.

8. Discussion and Conclusion

A transformer, asked for a token, spends most of its labour retrieving and selecting, and a small, scale-growing remainder computing something new. We have made that distinction measurable on real models, given it an exact geometric and causal characterisation, and shown that the same structure is a probabilistic logic, a tropical geometry, and an executable Datalog program according to a single degree of freedom, the temperature. The retrievable fragment is additive, lowrank, low-treewidth, and compactly verifiable; the irreducible computation is the diffuse, high-rank, dense-Gram region that no lookup table reproduces and no single module localises. The three interpretations are not analogies bolted together: they share one kernel G, one wall measured three ways, and one homomorphism (Maslov dequantization) between their temperatures.
