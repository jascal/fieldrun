Read: 'bits' = certified RELATIVE precision of the per-block decode contributions (b bits <-> tolerate 2^-b relative perturbation). Lower = more quantizable. Static = the single global bit-width (worst position); residue drops the hardest q% (forge-tax). Cashes in on every weight (bandwidth), unlike pruning. Worst-case is the certified guarantee; RMS is the realistic (independent-rounding) estimate. Mapping contribution-bits -> weight-bits is a separate, favorable step (a weight error spreads over the hidden dim).
